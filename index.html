Instructions

Flappy Princess — press SPACE or tap the object button to dash/up-flap. Avoid obstacles (they show the same object). Hitting an obstacle uses a heart or resets you to start; passing obstacles gives coins.

Princess Runner — Start triggers a 3–2–1 countdown. Click/SPACE to jump. Avoid obstacles. Coins on the track give coins.

Match Game — 12 cards (6 pairs). Flip two — matched pairs disappear and award coins. Clear the board to get bonus coins.

Draw-a-Path — Draw ahead of the moving world. The princess-car follows the drawn path. The world scrolls and new obstacles spawn; avoid them to collect coins.
